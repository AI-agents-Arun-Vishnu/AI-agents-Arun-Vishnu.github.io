Frequently Asked Questions

Will there be a free plan available?

No.

How will the pricing structure work?

Our pricing will be based on a per-user subscription model with different tiers based on feature access and usage limits. All plans will include our core AI scheduling capabilities.

Will there be special pricing for teams?

Absolutely! We'll offer team and enterprise plans with volume discounts and additional team management features. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise solutions.

Can I try Schedule.ai before subscribing?

Yes, we'll offer a 14-day free trial of our premium features so you can experience the full power of Schedule.ai before making a commitment.

Will I be able to change plans as my needs change?

Definitely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or adjust your plan at any time. We grow with your business and provide the flexibility you need.
